How to use it

  1. Open @pelikula_finder_bot in Telegram.
  2. 📎 Share your location, or send /setlocation City, Country.
  3. Send /cinemas for nearby cinemas, or /movies for what's on.
  4. Tap a film to see showtimes and buy tickets.
  5. Optional: /subscribe 09:00 for a daily push of what's playing.
